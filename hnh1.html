About Her and Her

As society's awareness of women's issues and the portrayal of women in films have evolved over time, female characters in movies have developed from one-dimensional stereotypes to more diverse social roles. Through their performances in movies, the actresses have led audiences to explore different female images and understand the various faces of women.

The online exhibition is a special exhibition that will showcase 30 actresses who have won the Golden Horse Award for Best Leading Actress from 1992 to 2022.
